About Us

A complete furniture and interior solutions company.

Wooden Hub is focused on quality work, clean finishing and customer satisfaction. With the tagline “Furniture That Feels Like Home,” we help customers design and improve their spaces with custom furniture, flooring, tiles, P.O.P., painting, lighting, wiring, grill work and turnkey interior services.

From furniture to full interior work, Wooden Hub helps create beautiful, comfortable and functional spaces that feel like home.

Our Services

Everything for your space, in one place.

From a single piece of furniture to complete interior work — Wooden Hub handles it all with care, quality materials and clean finishing.

  • Custom Furniture

    Design and make furniture for homes, offices, shops, bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens, wardrobes, TV units, tables, storage units and more.

  • Complete Interior Work

    Full interior work for residential and commercial spaces with proper planning, finishing and execution.

  • Wooden Flooring

    Stylish wooden flooring solutions that give spaces a warm, premium and elegant look.

  • Tiles Work

    Tiles work for floors, walls, kitchens, bathrooms, homes, offices, shops and renovation projects with clean finishing and proper alignment.

  • S.S. & Galvanized Grill

    Strong and stylish grill work for safety, durability and a modern appearance.

  • Aluminium Section

    Aluminium section work for windows, partitions, doors and other interior and exterior needs.

  • Invisible Grill

    Safe and modern invisible grill solutions for balconies, windows and open areas.

  • P.O.P. Work

    Ceiling, wall design, decorative P.O.P. work and finishing work for beautiful interiors.

  • Painting & Colour

    Interior and exterior painting with smooth finishing and attractive colour combinations.

  • Lighting & Wiring

    Electrical wiring and lighting setup to make the space functional, safe and beautiful.

  • Turnkey Interior Projects

    Complete project service from planning to final finishing — customers get all interior work done in one place.
